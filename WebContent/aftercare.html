Tattoo Aftercare

  1. Leave bandage on for a minimum of two hours.

  2. Gently remove bandage under warm running water to reduce risk of damaging the tattoo.

  3. Using an unscented soap, wash with your hand using warm soapy water. Do not re-bandage.

  4. Pat dry and wait 10 to 15 minutes before you apply the ointment.

  5. Apply the ointment as you would a hand lotion; rub it in. Do not leave any extra on the surface of your skin.

  6. Repeat step 5, four to six times per day. Do not apply ointment with out washing it first. Switch to hand lotion after 7 days.

Do's And Don'ts

  • DO

    use unscented lotion (Redemption or Aquaphor, After Inked, Curel, H2Ocean, Lubriderm).

  • DON'T

    pick or scratch tattoo while it is peeling.

  • DON'T

    soak the tattoo for 2 weeks.

  • DON'T

    Swim, take a bath (showers are ok) use a hottub or sauna for 2 weeks.

  • DON'T

    apply alcohol, vaseline, petroleum jelly or sun block on the fresh tattoo.

  • DON'T

    listen to friends or so called tattoo experts.
