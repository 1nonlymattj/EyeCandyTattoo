Meet Anthony Van Stratten

I have been tattooing for 9 years and counting! Tattooing came as an opportunity to me that I never dreamed I would have been able to achieve. And for that I thank my clients every day for their support and trust. I pride myself on having fun with the people that I work with, and providing a relaxed, communicative, and cleanly tattooing experience. I spent the intro of my career a little nomadic, working with many artists, and experiencing different shops, while I continued to push towards being around the people I enjoyed working with, and learning with, the most. I like to keep my artwork fun and fresh. I don’t like to settle into the same routines which is why I try to create artwork that I’d like to tattoo as frequently as possible. I’m always looking for fun projects no matter how busy my schedule is. I enjoy detail oriented work, vibrant color work, and line work based concepts that push for precision.
