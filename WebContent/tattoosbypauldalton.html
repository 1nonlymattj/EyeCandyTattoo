Meet Paul Dalton

I have been tattooing for 14 years, learning from other artists throughout my career has been a great help in shaping me into the artist I am today. I love to tattoo in general and I specialize in many forms of tattooing...I try not to lock myself down as a one trick pony so I am well rounded in many tattoo styles. I try to have as much fun as possible at my job with both the artwork and my clients, I really enjoy getting to know my clients and them me as well...I believe there should be a connection between myself and my clients because tattooing is a very personal experience and everyone involved should be comfortable on every level of the journey. If you would like a quality tattoo with a fun experience then get into my chair and let's create some beauty!
