Meet Lindsay Whitmore

My passion for tattooing started 13yrs ago when I started my apprenticeship. I then went towards receiving my Bachelors in Studio Arts at the University of Omaha. After graduating I joined the talented team at Eye Candy Tattoo where I have been for over 6 years. Learning and growing from my fellow tattoo artists. I enjoy tattooing many different styles and taking on the challenges of new ones. I specialize in Watercolor, fine line and illustrative detail. Vibrant colors are my go to. What I enjoy the most is taking a clients ideas and making it happen! My goals are to give my clients the best customer experience from their first consultation, through proofing their art to the time they spend in the shop getting tattooed. All things to make them comfortable and enjoy what can be a nervous experience.
